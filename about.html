Our Story: Nurturing Character from the Ground Up

Discover the heart behind Circles Of Character – our commitment to conscious parenting, science-backed methods, and the transformative power of nature for children aged 0-6.

Our 'Why': The Seed of an Idea

Circles Of Character grew from a deeply felt need – a desire to move beyond conventional early education and focus intentionally on what truly matters for lifelong flourishing: character.

We believe the foundational years, from birth to age six, are a precious window for nurturing the inner strengths that allow children to navigate life with resilience, empathy, and purpose. It's a time to cultivate not just cognitive skills, but the core qualities of a well-rounded human being.

Our approach thoughtfully blends evidence-based child development research – drawing inspiration from pioneers like Montessori and principles of secure attachment – with the undeniable, scientifically-proven benefits of deep immersion in the natural world. We aim to create a space where conscious parents feel supported in raising children who are not only knowledgeable but kind, curious, and connected.

What Makes Us Unique?

We offer a distinct approach designed to meet the needs of children and conscious parents in today's world.

Character-First Curriculum

Our core focus is intentionally cultivating traits like resilience and empathy through our unique 'Character Circles'.

Deep Nature Immersion

Nature *is* our classroom, fostering calm, focus, creativity, and a profound connection to the environment.

Science-Backed Methods

Our approach is grounded in child development psychology and educational best practices.

Small Group Sizes

We ensure personalized attention and foster strong, supportive relationships among children and facilitators.

Passionate Facilitators

Our team is trained in early childhood education and our specific character development framework.

Partnership with Parents

We believe in supporting and collaborating with parents as vital partners in their child's growth.

Our Mission

To empower children aged 0-6 with foundational character strengths through engaging, nature-based learning experiences, in partnership with conscious parents.

Our Vision

To cultivate a generation of resilient, compassionate, and purposeful individuals deeply connected to themselves, others, and the natural world.

Our Values

  • Growth: Fostering curiosity, learning, and the development of potential in children and adults.
  • Connection: Building strong relationships based on empathy, respect, and understanding.
  • Respect: Honouring each individual, the community, and the natural environment.
  • Integrity: Acting with honesty, fairness, and accountability in all we do.
  • Purpose: Guiding children towards understanding their unique place and potential contribution.
