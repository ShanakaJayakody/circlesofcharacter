Our Programs

Each program is carefully designed to be age-appropriate, fostering character development through meaningful experiences in nature.

Seedlings Circle (Ages 0-3)

A gentle introduction focusing on sensory exploration, secure attachment, early social awareness, and caregiver-child bonding in nature.

Key Features:

  • Caregiver participation required
  • Gentle rhythm with songs, stories, simple activities
  • Focus on trust, security, sensory awareness, connection

Sprouts Circle (Ages 3-6)

Developing independence, collaboration, problem-solving, and deeper character exploration through guided discovery and play.

Key Features:

  • Drop-off program
  • Project-based activities and peer interaction
  • Focus on resilience, empathy, curiosity, cooperation, self-regulation

A Glimpse Into Our Day

  1. Morning Arrival & Welcome Circle: Greeting friends, songs, setting intentions.
  2. Nature Exploration & Discovery: Free play and guided activities in our outdoor space.
  3. Shared Snack Time: Practicing mindfulness and social skills.
  4. Focused Character Activity: Engaging with the week's character theme through stories, crafts, or challenges.
  5. Story & Reflection Circle: Winding down, sharing discoveries, gratitude practice.
  6. Farewell & Transition: Preparing for home.
